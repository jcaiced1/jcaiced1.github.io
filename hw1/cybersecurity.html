Three key goals

  1. Confidentiality: only authorized users access data.
  2. Integrity: information remains accurate and unmodified.
  3. Availability: systems are accessible when needed.

Daily best practices

  • Use multi-factor authentication
  • Keep software patched
  • Back up important data
Real-world threat

Phishing remains one of the most common initial attack vectors in organizations.
