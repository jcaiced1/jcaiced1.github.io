Habits that reduce risk

  • Check sender addresses before opening links or files.
  • Never reuse the same password across multiple accounts.
  • Lock your laptop when you leave your desk.

User behavior matters

Most organizations improve security a lot just by training users regularly. Awareness programs help people recognize suspicious behavior and report potential incidents before damage grows.

Quick reminder

If something feels urgent and suspicious, verify it with a second source first. A quick confirmation through another channel can prevent account compromise and data loss.
