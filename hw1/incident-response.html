Response phases

Phase Purpose
Identify Confirm what happened and what was affected.
Contain Stop the spread by isolating devices or accounts.
Recover Restore services and monitor for recurring issues.

After the event

Document what happened, what worked, and what should be improved. Clear documentation helps teams react faster in future incidents and supports accountability across technical and non-technical roles.

Common output

A short report with timeline, impact, and next actions for the team. This report should include lessons learned and specific follow-up tasks with owners and deadlines.
