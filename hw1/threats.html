Main attacks

  • Phishing: fake emails or pages that try to collect credentials.
  • Malware: software used to spy, damage files, or open backdoors.
  • Ransomware: attackers encrypt files and demand money.

Why it works

Most attacks start with social engineering, not technical complexity.

Simple example

A fake message says your account expires today and asks you to click a link immediately.
