Basic checklist

  1. Use long, unique passwords in every account.
  2. Enable multi-factor authentication where possible.
  3. Keep operating systems and apps updated.
  4. Back up important files every week.

Defense strategy

The best protection usually combines technical controls, user training, and simple routines. Security improves a lot when teams apply these measures consistently instead of treating them as one-time tasks.

Practical note

Even one habit, like checking links before clicking, can prevent many incidents. Small repeated actions reduce mistakes and make social engineering attempts easier to detect.
