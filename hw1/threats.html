Common attack types

  • Phishing: fake messages that try to steal usernames and passwords.
  • Malware: malicious software that can spy on or damage systems.
  • Ransomware: attacks that lock files and demand payment.

Why attacks succeed

Many attacks target people first by using urgency, fear, or trust.

Example

A fake bank email asks you to click a link immediately to avoid account suspension.
