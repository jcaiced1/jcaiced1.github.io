Basic checklist

  1. Use long, unique passwords in every account.
  2. Enable multi-factor authentication where possible.
  3. Keep operating systems and apps updated.
  4. Back up important files every week.

Defense strategy

The best protection usually combines technical controls, user training, and simple routines.

Practical note

Even one habit, like checking links before clicking, can prevent many incidents.
